Game rule
It is simple browser game defense type.Your task is location of towers on the top to kill as many orcas as possible. At the start you receive 100 gold for purchasing two towers. Also you have 10 lives. For killing every orc you obtain additional 25 gold and 10 points, which is counted in top right corner. At the moment when you lost 10 lives in the game you lost. Monsters will apper 3 seconnd after starting. If you are ready. You can click "Start" button which is below this decription. Good luck!
